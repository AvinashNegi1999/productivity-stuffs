### ​​ Tips for Success
- ✅ **Pick a niche** and stick to it until you start earning.

- 🌐 **Network actively** — Reddit, Discord, LinkedIn groups.  
- 📈 **Work daily** — Devote 1–2 hours to learning and applying.
